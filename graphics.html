The Biscotti Company

Designed the paper bag packaging for a biscotti brand. They had three flavors, but only one packaging that consisted of only nutritional facts on the back and their logo on the front.

The bottom row is the first iteration of designs, indicating the flavors with colors and text, incorporating more of the brand.

The top row is the second iteration, with a more simplified design, indicating the flavors with images of the ingredients in each flavor and using thinner banners to bring more attention to the brand's identity.

Tropical Harvest


Updating their fruit juice e-commerce website to a more modern design, while keeping the elements of their brand that tells their story.

The website originally had too much content and very minimal white space, leaving the user overwhelmed. I simplified the text and reedited their images to bring more focus to the more important aspects of Tropical Harvest's brand and values.

They previously also had the products listed on a separate page and had no call to action to encourage the users to purchase their products. Thus, I added more buttons that would lead users to the product page and added image sliders with the products featured on the home page to make their products more known to the user.
