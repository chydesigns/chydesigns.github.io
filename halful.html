Halful

A Meditation and Mindfulness App

Duration
Summer 2016 (6 weeks)


Skills
User research, app development, prototyping, project management, and communication skills.


Tools
Sublime Text, Bootstrap

Overview

A mental-health awareness web-app that a team and I worked on at the end of our Girl's Who Code Summer Immersion program as our final project. Coded in HTML/CSS and JavaScript, also with use of Bootstrap.

The inspiration behind the app was to create an accessible way for people to cope with their stress and anxiety. The app allows the user to log in their emotions daily and tracks each user's progress. There are also options for the users to explore and find ways to improve their emotional state. The name of the app is to remind every user we should always try to view things positively and that the glass is always half full.

Reflections

This was my first experience working in a mobile environment and I learned a lot about how working on a team means having shared responsibility and not just a division of work.

Looking back on this project, I would have spent more time on user testing. Learning from my recent experiences, multiple iterations and testing always assures the delivery of positive results.
