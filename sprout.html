Sprout

A Google Design Exercise

Duration
Spring 2020 (2 weeks)


Skills
User research, prototyping, and project management

​
Tools
Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop

Overview

A prototype of an app idea I designed based on Google's 2020 UX/UI Design Prompt. I was inspired to create a social media platform to connect university students to mentors and mentees.

I enjoyed working on this project because the goal and purpose of the app aligned with my vision of the college experience. It should be a memorable experience with meaningful connections to your student body community. I considered the needs of the individual users that would interact with the app and further explained its functions in this presentation.

Reflections

While working on this project, I hoped to practice my design skills and gain more experience with understanding the user's point of view and centering my design based on their needs.

Reviewing this project, I would go back and spend more time on user research and create more accurate personas.

I would create a survey to gather real data on students at my university to better understand their pain points and design frameworks that really complimented their needs and creating a solution that would enhance their college experience.

Overall, this project was good practice and a very relatable topic. This could be a possible idea that could actually be implemented at my university.

Thank you for reading!
